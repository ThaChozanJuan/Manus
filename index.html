How this works

  1. Productivity savings/emp = salary × loss% × reduction%.
  2. Turnover savings/emp = turnover% × (replacement% × salary) × reduction%.
  3. Absenteeism savings/emp = days × reduction% × (salary ÷ 220).
  4. Manager time saved = managers × hours/mo × 12 × (mgrSalary ÷ 1980).
  5. Total savings = employees × adoption% × (sum of per-emp savings) + manager time saved.
  6. Program cost = plan price(ex-GST) × total employees.
  7. Net = savings − cost; ROI% = net ÷ cost.
  8. Break-even ≈ price(ex-GST) ÷ [(per-emp savings) + (manager-savings per-emp)].

Important: This calculator provides directional estimates only and is not financial advice. Results depend on your assumptions and context. All values shown in AUD; ROI uses ex-GST pricing for comparability.
